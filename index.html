The Story

Goldilocks was a curious girl who one day entered the house of three bears while they were away. She tasted their food, sat in their chairs, and fell asleep in one of their beds. When the bears returned, they noticed what had happened. Goldilocks woke up, got scared, and ran away. The bears learned to lock their door, and Goldilocks learned to respect others' space.
